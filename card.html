Red Kangaroo

Kangaroos live and travel in organized groups or "mobs," dominated by the largest male.

Binomial Name: Macropus rufus

Average Length: 1.3-1.6 meter(males)

Average Lifespan:6-8years

Habitat: lives in varied habitats(Forest,woodland,grassy plains and savannas)

Kangaroos are shy and retiring by nature, and in normal circumstances present no threat to humans. Male kangaroos often "box" amongst each other, playfully, for dominance, or in competition for mates.
